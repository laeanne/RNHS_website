STEM Strand

The Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) strand prepares students for careers in science, engineering, and technology with subjects like mathematics, physics, and computer science.

×
HUMSS Strand

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand focuses on subjects like literature, philosophy, psychology, and sociology, preparing students for careers in teaching, law, and public service.

×
ABM Strand

The Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand focuses on business concepts, financial management, and economics, preparing students for careers in business administration, accounting, and entrepreneurship.

×
GAS Strand

The General Academic Strand (GAS) offers a flexible curriculum, allowing students to explore subjects from various tracks such as business, humanities, and sciences, ideal for those who are undecided about their future career path.

×
TVL Strand

The Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) strand prepares students with hands-on skills in fields like culinary arts, automotive, electronics, and ICT, equipping them for immediate employment or entrepreneurship.

  • ICT (Information and Communications Technology)
    Learn the basics of programming, networking, and web development.
  • HE (Home Economics)
    Focus on skills like culinary arts, hospitality, and tourism management.
  • Dressmaking
    Gain skills in sewing, fashion design, and garment production.
  • SMAW (Shielded Metal Arc Welding)
    Learn welding techniques for various industries including construction and automotive repair.
